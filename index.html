Our story
Baked with Passion Since 2012

What started as a small home kitchen in Jaipur has grown into the city's most loved artisan bakery. We believe great baking is equal parts science and soul — precise recipes, the best butter, local flour, and hands that genuinely care about what comes out of the oven.

Every loaf, every cake, every croissant is made from scratch. No mixes, no shortcuts. Just real ingredients and real craft, baked fresh every single morning before the sun rises.

— Meera & Rajesh Sharma, Founders
